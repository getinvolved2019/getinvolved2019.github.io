Who we are

Get to know us and our motivations

A group of students

We are four students at the University of Warwick. Although we study really eclectic subjects, we all agree on one point : something has to be done about the environment, and it has to be done now. These are our main aims :

  • To spread awareness about the current situation.
  • To connect as many student around the world under the same cause.
  • To get big enough in order to be heard, and to make big change.
